ABOUT US

IEMPHYS-21 - International Conference in Advanced Physics is organized by Institute of Engineering & Management, Kolkata, India in association with IEM Society of Physics Student Chapter (AIP) and Smart Society, USA from 1st to 3rd April, 2021 in online platform.
After the successful execution of IEMPHYS-19 – International Conference in Condensed Matter Physics, this is the second venture of Department of Basic Science & Humanities, IEM in organising a conference in the Physics domain.
IEMPHYS-21 is an interdisciplinary forum on advanced topics of Physics like Solid State Physics, Nuclear Physics, High Energy Physics, Astrophysics, Computational Physics, Nano Science and Nano Technology, Biophysics, Optical system and photonic devices and Quantum Information Science.

The success of IEMPHYS-19 has motivated us to organise IEMPHYS-21 - an international platform for researchers, eminent scientists and academicians to exchange their research work and innovative ideas in different advanced domains in Physics. The conference aspires to exhibit scientific paper presentations and poster presentations by scientific minds besides talks and keynote lectures by renowned scientists round the globe.

Due to the global pandemic Covid -19 we have organised this year’s conference in digital platform keeping in mind the safety of all participants and speakers. We invite research paper submissions for oral and poster presentation. Abstracts of registered papers will be included in the conference abstract booklet. Full papers submitted will be published in International Journal of Innovative Research in Physics (IJIIP).

We hope IEMPHYS-21 will be a great learning opportunity and enriching experience for all participants. 

CALL FOR PAPERS

IEMPHYS-21 is a platform for presentations of research papers in different advanced domains in Physics. The conference invites submission of papers on the topics given below but not limited to:

Device modeling and simulation

Optical system and photonic devices

Solid state Physics

Astrophysics and cosmology

Biophysics and application

Quantum information science

NUCLEAR Physics

Nano-science and Nano-technology

High energy Physics

All full length papers submitted will be peer reviewed. Selected full length papers after peer review will be published.

PUBLICATION

Full papers submitted will be peer reviewed and published in International Journal of Innovative Research in Physics (IJIIP) Published by Smart Society, USA (Print ISSN : 2689-484X, online ISSN: 2687-7902).

• The deadline for abstract submission is 27/01/2021.
• Abstracts MUST be prepared in English.
